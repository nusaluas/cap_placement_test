Reading: My name is Alvaro

"My name is Alvaro. I am 7 years old. Every morning, I wake up at 6 o'clock. I eat breakfast with my family. I eat vegetables, fish and rice. After breakfast, I ride a motorcycle to school with my father. School starts at 8 o'clock. My father rides the bike slowly and safely. Every morning, we study English. I love English class! My headmaster teaches English. She is so kind and clever. I also love Science class! Science starts at 10 o'clock. My Science teacher is funny and smart. The whole class always enjoys Science class. I hope I can grow up to be a scientist one day! To do that, I must study hard!"

Reading: Frans from Australia

"My name is Frans and I come from Australia. I am 24 years old, and I live in a small town near Sydney called Branton. I don't have a job now, but normally I clean shop windows. I graduated from the Sydney Senior High School. I live with my mother and my sister. My father lives in Liverpool. We always call him every night. At the weekend, we like to go swimming in a big lake near my house. I normally get up at eight o'clock, but on Monday and Thursday, I get up at six o'clock because that is the day when I go running in the park. My father said to me when I was a child that exercise is good for my health. I believe he is proud of me. I can run for two hours, without stopping! I want to challenge myself for the next time. I want to run up to the mountain!"

Speaking Test (Examiner Tool)

Note: These are prompts for the teacher/examiner.

  • What is your name?
  • Where are you from?
  • How old are you?
  • Where is your hometown?
  • Where is your school?
  • Who is your best friend? (or What is his/her name?)
  • What is your mother/father’s job?
  • Which school subject do you like?
  • Do you have siblings (brother or sister)?
  • How old are your siblings?
  • What is your favorite animal? What can it do?
  • If the student passes these, proceed to free speaking/storytelling.
